MANAGING DIRECTOR
& PRINCIPAL ADVISOR

SHAHEEL HOODA

Shaheel is a former strategy consultant with McKinsey and Company, the world’s most reputed management consulting firm to the Fortune 500.

Following this, he applied his expertise to more than a hundred entrepreneurial companies over the past 25 years. Most recently he been involved helping a high tech software company grow globally, achieve a valuation of in excess of $50 million, and assisted in raising $20 million – all in a short 5 year period.

Shaheel holds an MBA from Harvard Business School and a Bachelor of Commerce (Distinction) from the University of Alberta. In 2016 he was awarded an Entrepreneur Promotion award from Startup Canada for the support he provides to growth companies.

An active community builder, he served as Chairman of the A100+ Entrepreneurs Organization, is a University of Alberta Venture Services Mentor, and a Harvard Innovation Lab Advisor. He also joined forces with other community builders and launched Sprout Fund LP to encourage the growth of a capital market in Alberta for early stage technology startups.
